Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos

Descripion

Simple to make, yet tantalising chicken tacos!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons of taco seasoning
  • 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Steps and Directions

  1. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl.
  2. Place chicken in your favorite slow cooker, pouring the broth mixture over the chicken
  3. cook on low setting for 6 to 8 hours. Shred the chicken after.
  4. Enjoy with desired toppings and add-ons!
